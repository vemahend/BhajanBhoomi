What is Bhajan Bhoomi?

Bhajan Bhoomi is a place where devotees gather to sing devotional songs (bhajans) and express their devotion.

Benefits of Bhajans

  • Brings peace of mind
  • Purifies the soul
  • Strengthens devotion
